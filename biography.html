J-Slow

JEUNE SLOW, BORN IN 1989 IN PARIS, BEGAN WRITING HIS FIRST LYRICS AT THE TENDER AGE OF 16. INSPIRED BY THE R’N’B, SOUL AND POP OF THE 90s  AND EARLY 2000s, SLOW'S MOST NOTABLE INFLUENCES INCLUDE NOTORIOUS BIG, 2PAC, JAY-Z, SNOOP DOGG,  KANYE WEST, LIL WAYNE, DIPSET, MAX B & WIZ KHALIFA, AMONG OTHERS.

JEUNE SLOW THEN DISCOVERED THE EMERGING URBAN SCENE AROUND HIM, AND STARTED TO FOLLOW THE WORK OF FELLOW FRENCH ARTISTS LIKE DOC GYNECO, ARSENIK, LUNATIK & IAM.

IT WAS IN 2010, THAT HE BEGAN COLLABORATING WITH OTHER MUSICIANS;  CREATING HIS FIRST PROJECT WITH THE BEATMAKER,  SRABIMACHINE AND THE SINGER, JOY THOMASS.

INTEREST IN J- SLOW'S WORK BEGAN TO BUILD UPON THE 2011 RELEASE OF THE MIXTAPE “LE JOUR J”.

CONFIRMATION OF HIS TALENT WAS SOLIDIFIED IN THE FOLLOWING MIXTAPE, “TAPIS ROUGE”. HERE, HIS WORK WAS SHOWCASED, ATTRACTING AN AUDIENCE INTERESTED IN HIS UNIQUE AND DISTINCT MUSICALITY.

ONLINE MAGAZINES LIKE “LES INROCKUPTIBLES”, “SOUL-ADDICT”, “OFIVE”, & “YARD” IMMEDIATELY SUPPORTED THE RAPPER'S CRAFT.

FINALLY, IN 2014, SLOW RELEASED THE EP, “NEW WAVE”. JEUNE SLOW IS NOW COMING BACK WITH HIS LATEST PROJECT “CASH MIRE” ENTIRELY PRODUCED BY THE BEATMAKER SRABIMACHINE.
