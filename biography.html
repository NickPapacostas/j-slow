--- layout: default title: Biography ---
J-Slow is an artist who was born in Paris in 1989. He started to write his first songs at sixteen years old. His youth was influenced by the afro american music (Rap, Rnb, Soul & Pop).

After he met two of his close friends, Srabimachine (beatmaker) and Joy Thomass (Singer) J-Slow dropped his first mixtape in 2011 named "Le Jour J" this tape was more like a training tape but it was with his 2nd mixtape "Tapis Rouge" that the media started to get interest on his work. He had a distribution deal with the label Believe for his 1st ep "New Wave" in 2014 and he's about to release his second ep soon.
